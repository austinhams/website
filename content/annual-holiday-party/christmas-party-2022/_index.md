# Join us for our Face-To-Face, In-Person Christmas/Holiday Party!

\[vc\_column\_text responsive\_text=”true” font\_size=”22px”\]Yes, the famous Austin Amateur Radio Club Christmas Party is back, with all the usual contests, recognition awards and plenty of door prizes! There’ll be lots of fun and socializing, too!

**To celebrate our getting together, the club is covering the cost of a buffet dinner (entree, appetizer & non-alcoholic beverage\*) for club members and your spouse/significant other!**

[The Buffet King options feature a wide variety of tasty food choices](https://buffetkingaustintexas.com/23875).

**So, yes: Free Food! 😉  Sign Up at the bottom of this page!**

\* Hard drinks are the responsibility of each person ordering them, along with appropriate gratuity for drink service.

**(If you’re not yet a club member, [now would be a great time to become one!)](/join/)**

The QLF contest (morse code with your left foot, or non-dominant foot, Lew!) will again take place, with the winner receiving the highly-coveted QLF trophy!

Our top door prize this year is a [Yaesu FTM-6000R Dual Band UHF/VHF 50W Radio](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/FTM-6000R_E_Catalog_web.pdf), with removable faceplate and available Bluetooth , (a $300 value!), which will be awarded to one lucky winner.\[/vc\_column\_text\]

## White Elephant Gift Exchange!

\[vc\_column\_text responsive\_text=”true” font\_size=”22px”\]Something new and fun this year!

It works like this:

1.  Bring something that’s wrapped and ham radio related, worth at least $10, and works or is usable just like it is.
2.  Get a numbered ticket for the exchange drawing.
3.  When your ticket number is called, either select a remaining gift and open it, or “steal” a previously opened gift. But a “theft” is only allowed once per recipient. If your selection is “stolen”, you immediately get a new selection.

Gifts might include a cheap used radio, a vacuum tube, antenna wire or insulators, a book, a microphone, a hand tool, etc. Homebrew items are OK if they work.

Artistic creations could be entered too, but they must meet the requirements in number 1 above.

So, what’ya got?? :)\[/vc\_column\_text\]
