We are proud to recognize the outstanding individuals selected as recipients of the **2025 Greenwood Scholarship**. These operators have demonstrated dedication, curiosity, and a commitment to advancing their knowledge and skills within the amateur radio community.

This year’s recipients represent a wide range of experience levels, from those just beginning their journey to those achieving the highest levels of licensing. Each of them reflects the spirit of learning, service, and technical excellence that the Greenwood Scholarship was created to support.
