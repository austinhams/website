In December, we take a break from our regular monthly meeting and instead gather for our annual Holiday Party. This special event gives us a chance to relax, enjoy time together, and celebrate another great year of amateur radio fellowship. It’s also when we hold our officer elections, helping set the course for the club in the year ahead. We look forward to seeing everyone there and appreciate all our members who continue to make this club thrive.
