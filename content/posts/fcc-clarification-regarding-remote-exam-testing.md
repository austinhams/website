Per the FCC’s Public Notice released April 30, 2020. “**We make clear here that nothing in the FCC’s rules prohibits remote testing, and prior FCC approval is not required to conduct remote tests.**”  So… Remote testing IS allowed!  Full text here: [https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DA-20-467A1.pdf](https://docs.fcc.gov/public/attachments/DA-20-467A1.pdf)
