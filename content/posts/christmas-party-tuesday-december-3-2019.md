In lieu of our regular membership meeting, the Austin Amateur Radio Club invites you to join us for our annual **Christmas Party**!

**When:** Tuesday, December 3, 2019. 6:00pm (festivities and such to start about 7pm)

**Where:**  Buffet King, 5451 N Interstate Hw 35, Austin TX 78723 (Capitol Plaza, just north of 51st Street)

Attendees purchase their buffet meal as they enter. We’ll have the large meeting room to the right after you enter. The staff can help direct you. Visit [https://buffetkingaustintexas.com/](https://buffetkingaustintexas.com/)

Raffle tickets will be available for Door Prizes. A **QLF** CW contest will be held. Ham Of The Year award will be presented, along with the usual frivolity. A special **100th Year Anniversary** presentation will be made by Joe Fisher, K5EJL. Family, Friends, Non-members are all welcome! For more information, contact Lori, KM5MQ@ARRL.net
