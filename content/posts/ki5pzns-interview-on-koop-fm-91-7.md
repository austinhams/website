On Wednesday, February 7, I was interviewed by Mindy Reed at KOOP FM 91.7 radio here in Austin, Texas, on the occasion of UNESCO World Radio Day, coming up this Tuesday, February 13, talking about Ham Radio.

The theme this year for World Radio Day is “Radio: A century informing, entertaining and educating.” [Here’s the link for more information about World Radio Day.](https://radio.co/blog/world-radio-day#:~:text=The%20theme%20of%20World%20Radio,promise%20of%20a%20dynamic%20future.)

I hope that I was able to encourage more people to become licensed Hams. Even the host was excited about ham radio!
