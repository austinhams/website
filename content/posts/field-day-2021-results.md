The 2021 American Radio Relay League (ARRL) annual Field Day was held June 26-June 27, involving several hours of setup on Saturday, 24 hours of operating and several hours of teardown on Sunday. We operated at our usual site, the Austin Chapter of the American Red Cross, while observing their pandemic safety guidelines. While Field Day was a bit attenuated this year due to the pandemic, it was still reasonably well-attended and ran smoothly. Thanks to special rules for this year, we were able to add the QSO results from several home shacks to our club totals. [Results are here.](/history-2/)
