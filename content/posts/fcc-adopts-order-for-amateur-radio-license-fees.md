On December 23, 2020, FCC adopted the Report and Order for a new application fee schedule which includes Amateur Radio licensees. The initially suggested fee of $50 has been reduced. Per the Order, Paragraph 32, “_We agree with commenters asserting this fee is too high to account for the minimal staff involvement in these applications and therefore adopt a reduced amount of $35 fee for all personal license application fees._“

nn

The effective date has not been decided yet, and is not official until published in the Federal Register.
