# Field Day 2021

The 2021 American Radio Relay League (ARRL) Field Day is June 26-June 27.

Actual on-air time is from 1 p.m. Central Saturday to 1 p.m. Central Sunday, a full 24 hours, with setup beforehand on Saturday and teardown afterwards on Sunday, both of which will be relatively minimal this year.

We will be having a restricted in-person Field Day this year at the [Austin Chapter of the American Red Cross, 2218 Pershing Dr, Austin, TX 78723](https://www.google.com/maps/place/American+Red+Cross/@30.286748,-97.7013481,16z/data=!4m9!1m2!2m1!1sred+cross!3m5!1s0x0:0x36da899764cc690e!8m2!3d30.287368!4d-97.6984092!15sCglyZWQgY3Jvc3MiA4gBAZIBF25vbl9wcm9maXRfb3JnYW5pemF0aW9u).

The Red Cross requires that we limit the number of attendees on site at any one time. As such, we are asking you to schedule when you will be there for Field Day.

In addition, in order to handle the date/time scheduling, we’ve had to split our Field Day into four different pieces:

-   [Saturday Setup](https://calendly.com/austinhams/aarc-field-day-2021-saturday-setup-hours)
-   [Saturday Operating Hours](https://calendly.com/austinhams/aarc-field-day-2021-saturday-operating-hours)
-   [Sunday Operating Hours](https://calendly.com/austinhams/aarc-field-day-2021-sunday-operating-hours)
-   [Sunday Teardown](https://calendly.com/austinhams/aarc-field-day-2021-sunday-teardown-hours)

If you don’t have a license yet, or are not comfortable Operating, please consider signing up  to help with Setup and Teardown. There’s a lot of fun to be had at Field Day outside of Operating, and it’s a great opportunity to meet your fellow Ham Club members and learn new things.

Also, if you sign-up for Operating Hours, please also consider signing-up to help with [Setup](https://calendly.com/austinhams/aarc-field-day-2021-saturday-setup-hours) and [Teardown](https://calendly.com/austinhams/aarc-field-day-2021-sunday-teardown-hours).

Lastly, once you schedule your time(s), please contact your [Captain.](/#Captains)

Upon arrival at the Red Cross on each day of Field Day, please check in with the Safety Officer first, and then your [Captain](/#Captains) upon arrival.

We have access to Building A (Radio Room), Building B (Classrooms A & B) and the parking lot. Our Emergency Communications (EMCOMM) trailer will be in the Red Cross parking lot.

We are under American Red Cross (ARC) and Centers for Disease Control (CDC) Covid-19 protocols, and AARL rules and guidelines.
