Welcome to the **Meeting Archives** for the Austin Amateur Radio Club. These archives capture the full experience of our monthly meetings — important club business, in-depth technical presentations, and the practical knowledge shared by our talented members.

The archive is updated a few days after each Monthly Meeting; relevant slide-decks, handouts, and other reference files are posted alongside the meeting video as they become available.
