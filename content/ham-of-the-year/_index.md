# Ham of the Month

Each year, the Austin Amateur Radio Club (AARC) presents the **Ham of the Year Award** to an individual whose exceptional dedication, service, and spirit have made a lasting impact on the amateur radio community. This honor recognizes outstanding contributions that go above and beyond in advancing the hobby, supporting club activities, promoting emergency preparedness, and strengthening our shared mission as amateur radio operators.

Do you know someone whose leadership, commitment, or technical expertise truly stands out? Someone who exemplifies what amateur radio is all about and has made a significant difference to the club or the wider community?

Nominate them for the AARC **Ham of the Year** Award.  
Please include a description of why you believe they deserve this recognition in your message.

Send your nomination to: **president [at] austinhams.org** (remove the spaces and brackets).
