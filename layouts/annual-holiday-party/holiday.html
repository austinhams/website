Annual Holiday Party

A night of food, fellowship, and 73s — our favorite way to close out the year together.

Every year, our Holiday Party brings together the entire club for a night of celebration, fun, and fellowship. It’s our chance to step away from the radios, relax, and enjoy each other’s company as we wrap up another incredible year of service, learning, and community. Whether you’ve been a member for decades or just joined this month, this event is for everyone.

The evening is filled with warmth, laughter, and plenty of holiday spirit — great food, festive music, and all the traditions that make this gathering something our members look forward to every year. From stories of Field Day and emergency deployments to new ham radio adventures, the conversations alone make the night unforgettable.

It’s also our way to honor the volunteers, operators, and leaders who keep our community strong, resilient, and connected throughout the year. Because of your dedication, our club continues to grow in skill, impact, and spirit. This event is our way of saying thank you.

So dust off your ugly holiday sweater, bring your family, and get ready for an evening filled with joy and togetherness. We can’t wait to celebrate the season with you!
