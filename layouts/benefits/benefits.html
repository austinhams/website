The voice of Amateur Radio in Central Texas

AARC and ARRL are the voice of Amateur Radio in Central Texas, the Hill Country, and nationally. Together we can protect our frequency allocations, serve our community, and support technological ingenuity.

General meetings are held on Zoom at 7:00 p.m. on the first Tuesday of each month (except December, for the holiday party). Dates are occasionally adjusted around holidays — check the events calendar for changes. We send the Zoom link and meeting info to our Groups.io mailing list each month.

Ready to join?

Club membership starts at $36/year. Higher tiers are available if you'd like to contribute more — dues are 100% tax deductible.
