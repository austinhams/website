What to expect

Whether you're a seasoned operator, newly licensed, or simply curious about amateur radio, you'll find our meetings friendly, informative, and welcoming. Each meeting blends club updates with high-quality technical content.

  • 1

    Club Business & Updates

    What's happening in the club and what's coming up next.

  • 2

    Solar Weather Report

    Current conditions and what they mean for propagation.

  • 3

    Feature Presentation

    In-depth technical talks on operating techniques, emergency communications, antennas, digital modes, RF theory, and more — practical and educational for operators at all experience levels.
