Less than a cup of coffee. Priceless when it matters.

We offer multiple membership levels for one simple reason: so everyone can participate at a level that works for them. There is no difference in access, voice, or value between tiers — choose what you're comfortable contributing.

For years, our club's infrastructure has existed because dedicated members stepped up. Repeaters, antennas, and systems were built, repaired, and maintained with personal time, personal tools, and personal money. That generosity is the foundation of what we have today.

But infrastructure ages. Equipment fails. Parts become harder to source. If we want to remain dependable — both day-to-day and when emergencies strike — we need to be proactive together. Your monthly contribution helps us maintain and repair the aging repeater system, plan for upgrades and responsible expansion, and improve coverage and reliability across the region.

This isn't about profit or status. It's about stewardship — taking care of what we've built together and ensuring it's still there for the next operator who keys up.
