Whether you’re upgrading your shack or looking for a deal, here’s how to get involved.

  1. 1

    Tune in

    Sunday at 9:00 PM CT on 146.940 MHz (107.2 Hz tone).

    Check in

    Got something to buy, sell, or trade? Check in with the net control and announce your item.
